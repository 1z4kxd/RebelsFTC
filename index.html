Who We Are?
Rebels 23007 is a First Tech Challenge robotics team community-based in Zhanaozen, Kazakhstan. First is a global nonprofit inspiring students to become future leaders in STEM through unique, hands-on programs. Each year, we build, design, and program robots to play games released by FIRST and spread our passion for innovation through outreach. We currently have 9 students in grades 9 to 10 and adult mentor
